Outreach & Engagement

I enjoy participating in outreach activities aimed at bringing computer science and technology closer to students and the general public. I regularly collaborate with schools, museums, and educational institutions through workshops, demonstrations, and public events.

Museum Activities

I'm the Deputy Director for Education and Activities at the Museum of Computing (UPV), where I coordinate the workshops and guided visits the museum runs throughout the year. These activities bring computer science closer to a broad and diverse audience, with a special focus on children and teenagers, fostering STEM vocations through hands-on workshops on programming, robotics, cryptography, and the history of computing. I also participate in the organization of Retrópolis, an event regarding retro games and retro computers.
