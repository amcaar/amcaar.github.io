Teaching

I have been teaching at the Universitat Politècnica de València (UPV) since 2013, across several schools and both undergraduate and graduate levels — from introductory programming to cloud and high-performance computing. My courses have been taught in Spanish, Valencian, and English, and my teaching survey results have consistently been excellent (average of ~9).
