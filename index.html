Hi! I'm Amanda Calatrava

I'm an assistant professor at the Department of Computer Systems and Computation (DSIC) at the Universitat Politècnica de València (UPV). My research focuses on cloud computing, serverless computing, and AI applied to science — basically, I spend my time thinking about how to make scientific computing more agile, scalable, and accessible to those who need it.

I got my PhD from the UPV in 2016 (cum laude, see my thesis here), and I've been part of the GRyCAP research group ever since, working on European projects like ATMOSPHERE, EOSC-Synergy, and AI4EOSC. I'm the (co)author of several papers in international journals and conferences, and I still learn something new almost every week. You can check out all my publications here.
